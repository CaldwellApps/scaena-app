What is Scaena?

Scaena is a premium, privacy-focused iOS and macOS tracking application. It allows you to explore trending TV shows and movies, organize your media watchlist, monitor upcoming show release dates, view airing schedules, and synchronize your watch history seamlessly—all protected behind fully native spoiler controls.

Does Scaena track my personal data?

No. Scaena is built on a 100% local database model using Apple's secure Core Data container. Your settings and API keys sync exclusively via your personal Apple iCloud Key-Value store. No external servers or third-party tracking databases collect your personal data.

What languages does Scaena support?

Scaena is fully localized into 22 global languages: English, Spanish, Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), Japanese, French, German, Portuguese (Portugal & Brazil), Italian, Korean, Russian, Arabic, Dutch, Swedish, Norwegian, Danish, Hindi, Turkish, Indonesian, Vietnamese, and Polish. You can customize the interface language directly in the app settings.

How does Scaena avoid TV show episode spoilers?

Scaena features a native "Hide Episode Spoilers" toggle. When enabled, episode descriptions are hidden behind a spoiler alert, and specific episode thumbnails are replaced with general season posters until you mark the episode as watched.

How do I sync my watchlist with Simkl?

Simply navigate to settings inside the app and authorize your Simkl account. Scaena's background SyncManager executes all API calls and database commits asynchronously on dedicated threads, ensuring your watchlists, ratings, and history remain perfectly in sync without ever causing frame rate drops.

What metadata services does Scaena use?

Scaena fetches show schedules, user lists, and poster art from Simkl, and high-resolution episode backdrops from TheTVDB (TVDB). All metadata lookups are handled automatically using secure, integrated developer access keys.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 13, 2026

1. Overview

Scaena is designed with user privacy in mind. We do not run any remote user tracking, analytics, or profiling servers. Your data belongs entirely to you.

2. Information We Process

We process credentials and metadata necessary to run the service:

  • Simkl API Credentials: Securely stored on-device.
  • Media Watchlist & Rating Data: Retrieved and cached locally.
  • App Store Purchases: Verification details are processed securely by Apple. Scaena does not collect or store your payment details.
  • System logs: Transient error logs kept locally for diagnostics.

3. Storage and Syncing

Your watchlist, lists, and settings are stored locally on your device. We utilize Apple iCloud Synchronization to securely backup and sync your settings across your own iOS/macOS devices. We do not have access to your iCloud container or your personal data.

4. Third-Party API Data Sharing

To synchronize your media watch history, Scaena communicates directly with the Simkl API. When requesting artwork and metadata, the app communicates with Simkl and TheTVDB. Any information shared with these services is governed strictly by their respective privacy policies.

5. Security

We use industry-standard security protocols to transmit data over secure HTTPS connections. Your access token is kept in secure local storage or Keychain services.

6. Your Choices & Deletion

You can disconnect your Simkl account at any time via the Settings menu, which immediately deletes Simkl tokens and credentials stored inside the application database. Since all other data resides in iCloud, deleting the app will remove local storage caches.
